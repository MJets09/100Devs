Deep Dish Brownies

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup butter
  • melted 1 1/2 cups
  • white sugar 1 1/2
  • teaspoons vanilla extract 3 eggs 3/4 cup
  • all-purpose flour 1/2 cup
  • unsweetened cocoa powder 1/2
  • teaspoon baking powder 1/2
  • teaspoon salt

Directions

60 mins Servings 9

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and Grease an 8 inch square pan.
  2. In a large bowl, blend melted butter, sugar and vanilla. Beat in eggs one at a time.
  3. Combine the flour, cocoa, baking powder and salt. Gradually blend into the egg mixture.
  4. Spread the batter into the prepared pan.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 40 to 45 minutes, or until brownies begin to pull away from the sides of the pan.
  6. Let brownies cool, then cut into squares.

Enjoy! Tip Aluminum foil can be used to keep food moist, cook it evenly, and make clean-up easier.
